Corner Bracket Lover

By digital on Dec 12, 2012

Corner Bracket Lover is a WordPress plugin to convert all curly quotation marks (“” and ‘’) in your posts to traditional corner brackets (「」 and 『』). A must-have plugin if you're a Chinese writer or just a corner brackets lover. This plugin also works fine with multisite enabled WordPress (aka. WordPress Mu).
